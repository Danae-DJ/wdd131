Our Purpose

The purpose of the page is to help people, regardless of their age, choose a bicycle appropriate to the activities around them. There are people who enjoy cycling or want to learn, but do not know the types of bicycles and which ones are better as a sport or simply as a Hobby, whether in the country or in the city. So we share with you a brief description of cycling along with basic and relevant information.
